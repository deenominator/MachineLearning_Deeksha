Google Developer Group on Campus (GDGC) - Information Document

About GDGC:
Google Developer Group on Campus are university-based community groups for
students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all
undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are
welcome. By joining a GDGC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer
learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their communities.
It is open to any student, ranging from novice developers who are just starting, to
advanced developers who want to further enhance their skills. It is intended to be a
platform for students to learn and collaborate as they solve problems around them
with the help of technology.

Opportunities GDGC Provides:
- Learn developer technologies through workshops and events.
- Gain industry-like experience by solving real-world problems.
- Showcase prototypes and solutions to the community and industry leaders.
- Get inspired by success stories and learn from peers.
- Participate in global competitions such as Google Solution Challenge.

Vision:
To create a community of passionate developers who learn, grow, collaborate, and use technology to solve real-world problems.

Mission:
To provide resources, mentorship, and opportunities for students to grow as developers and build a strong tech community.

PAST EVENTS

1) Tech Winter Break (Online Two-Day Event)
Tech Winter Break 2024 is a two-day online tech fest hosted by GDGC VIT Bhopal. It includes hands-on workshops, expert guidance, and preparation for the Google Solution Challenge 2025.

Day 1: Web Development (14th Dec, 1:30 PM – 3:30 PM)
- Learn to build web apps using React and Flask.
- Explore modern web development tools and frameworks.
- Understand UI/UX design principles.

Day 2: Flutter Apps (15th Dec, 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM)
- Learn Flutter and Dart.
- Build cross-platform mobile applications.
- Understand widgets and mobile UI comp

Takeaways:
- Build real web and mobile apps.
- Receive certificates and exclusive resources.
- Get mentorship from industry experts.
- Prepare for the Google Solution Challenge.

Why Join?

1. Learn by Doing: Master industry-leading tools like React, Node.js, and Flutter
to build next-gen web and mobile apps.
2. Get Ahead: Boost your portfolio with certificates and exclusive resources, and
stay one step ahead with guidance from tech experts.
3. Connect and Collaborate: Meet fellow tech enthusiasts, share ideas, and find
your next collaborator or mentor.
4. Shape Your Future: Prepare yourself for real-world challenges and
opportunities, including Google’s global competition.

Who Should Join?
Whether you're:
1. A beginner ready to take your first steps in tech.
2. A student looking to level up your skills.
3. A developer eager to explore modern frameworks.
4. If you’ve got the passion, we’ve got the platform.

What You’ll Gain
1. Day 1: Learn to create unforgettable web apps using React and Node.js—turn
your ideas into digital art.
2. Day 2: Master Flutter to build stunning, cross-platform apps that feel at home
on any device.
3. Certifications, practical knowledge, and expert guidance to take your tech
journey to the next level.
4. A head start in Google’s global challenge and the tools to build something
extraordinary.

What You’ll Need
1. A laptop or desktop with a reliable internet connection.
2. Basic programming know-how

Why Wait?

This isn’t just another online event; it’s a two-day experience designed to help you
unlock your potential, meet like-minded innovators, and embrace the future of
development—all without leaving your cosy corner.

2) Google Olympics 2k25
Google Olympics 2k25 is GDGC’s flagship competition where teams of four represent countries and compete in technical and strategic challenges. The event involves logic puzzles, coding challenges, teamwork tasks, and unexpected plot twists. Winners earn campus-wide recognition.

-------------------------------------------

3) Build With AIs – VITB Edition
Build With AIs is part of a global series aimed at helping developers learn AI and machine learning tools from Google. The event includes interactive workshops, collaborations, hands-on challenges, and project showcases. It is suitable for beginners and experienced coders.
